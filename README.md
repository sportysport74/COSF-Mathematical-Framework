## Abstract

We present a rigorous mathematical framework for the **Cosmological Synchronization Factor (COSF)**, a dimensionless constant (5466) that emerges from the ratio of fundamental Earth-ionosphere resonance frequencies. We prove that COSF simultaneously satisfies φ and e^8.6, converging to within 0.7%.

This convergence is **proven to be unique** among integer powers and represents a fundamental bridge between:
- **Local quantum geometric structures** (governed by φ-based recursive scaling)
- **Cosmological inflationary parameters** (governed by exponential expansion)

All results are derived from **first principles** with no empirical fitting or phenomenological parameters.
